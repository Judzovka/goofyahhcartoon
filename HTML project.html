Sano Manjiro - Mikey

About Mikey

Mikey is a side character from tokyo revengers. Plays a key role in the whole story. He is a charismatic and powerful gang leader.

Mikey's characteristics

  1. He smiles a lot
  2. His friends mean the world to him
  3. Sometimes falls into darkness and hurts everyone

Mikey's friends

Mikey is the most popular character in the show. However, he can only trust the founders of toman, his true friends. His best friend is most likely Draken because he is always with him
